Lance Treviño

Composer · Studio Engineer · Educator

Lance Treviño is an award-winning composer, studio engineer, and educator with professional experience across film, television, and video game scoring.

As CEO and lead composer of 3 Wines Music, Lance works with a wide range of collaborators to create thoughtful, cinematic, and story-driven music for visual media.

His work reflects a practical understanding of production, collaboration, and storytelling, bringing together composition, technology, and post-production experience.
